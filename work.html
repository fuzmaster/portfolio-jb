PASSIVE MEDIA GENERATION

AI-Assisted Video Pipelines

Manually cutting reels and animating subtitles for 50+ annual podcast episodes was throttling audience growth. I built custom React and Remotion scripts to automate subtitle generation end-to-end. By designing an LLM architecture that ingests raw transcripts and identifies high-retention clips, I established a passive pipeline that automatically generates full promotional media kits — driving audiences across a 100K+ subscriber network with zero manual editing.

  • React / Remotion
  • LLM Transcript Ingestion
  • Auto Social Kits
  • 100K+ Subscriber Network

11,000+ PROFESSIONAL AUDIENCE

Studio Architecture

To launch a live-streamed industry news show, I designed and configured a multi-camera studio environment from the ground up. I routed a network of Blackmagic cameras through a centralized Mac Mini switching station, managing both physical set construction and the digital broadcast feed to consistently engage an audience of over 11,000 professionals per episode.

  • Blackmagic Camera Network
  • ATEM Switching
  • Live Broadcast Engineering
  • Set Construction
